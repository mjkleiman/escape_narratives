Last weekend, Harry and Danielle were walking their dog Maddy through the park. They ran into some old friends that they hadn't seen in years. They struck up a long conversation about their lives, and decided to all go out to dinner on Thursday at Luigi's. However, it wasn't until after they said goodbye to their friends that they noticed that Maddy had gotten out of her leash! The couple searched throughout the whole park, calling out her name and getting more worried the longer they searched. He checked the nearby school and playground, and she investigated the baseball and soccer fields. Eventually, they found her: she was playing with a stick she found underneath a bench near where they stopped to talk with their friends.
